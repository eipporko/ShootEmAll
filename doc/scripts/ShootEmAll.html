Instructions

The goal of the game is to survive as long as you can.
When you kill a monster you can obtain cash and buy power ups or buildings.

Controls
W: Forward
S: Back
A: Left
D: Right
Left Mouse Button: Fire Weapon
P: Debug mode
1-2: Set zoom
Esc: Exit
